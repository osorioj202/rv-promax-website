Installation and Usage Guide for Berkey Countertop Water Filters in RVs

Step 1: Unbox and Inspect

  • Check all parts: upper and lower chambers, filter elements, spigot, wing nuts.

Step 2: Assemble the Filter

  • Attach the spigot to the lower chamber.
  • Insert and prime the Black Berkey filter elements (soak and flush as per instructions).
  • Place the upper chamber on top of the lower chamber.

Step 3: Fill and Filter

  • Pour untreated water into the upper chamber.
  • Allow gravity to pull water through the filters into the lower chamber.
  • Dispense filtered water from the spigot.

Step 4: Placement in RV

  • Place on a stable, flat countertop or table.
  • Ensure easy access to the spigot for filling bottles or cooking.

Maintenance and Care Tips for Longevity and Performance

  • Clean Chambers Monthly: Use mild soap and water; avoid harsh chemicals.
  • Filter Element Care: Prime new filters before use; clean with a Scotch-Brite pad if flow slows.
  • Replace Filters Timely: Monitor water taste and flow rate; replace filters every 3,000 gallons or sooner if needed.
  • Store Properly: When not in use, dry thoroughly and store in a clean, dry place.
