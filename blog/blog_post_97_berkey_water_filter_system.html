1. What is the Berkey Water Filter System?

The Berkey Water Filter System is a gravity-fed water purification system renowned for its ability to remove bacteria, viruses, heavy metals, chemicals, and other contaminants without electricity or plumbing. Designed for portability and durability, Berkey filters are perfect for RV owners who need a reliable source of clean water on the road.

2. How Berkey Water Filters Work: Technical Overview

Berkey systems use proprietary Black Berkey purification elements that combine microfiltration and adsorption technologies. Water passes through these elements by gravity, removing:

  • 99.9999% of bacteria and viruses
  • Heavy metals like lead, mercury, and arsenic
  • Chlorine, pesticides, herbicides, and VOCs
  • Sediment and turbidity

Each Black Berkey filter element can purify up to 3,000 gallons before replacement, making it cost-effective for long-term RV use.

6. Installation Guide: Step-by-Step Setup in Your RV

  1. Choose Location: Select a flat, stable surface near your kitchen or water access point.
  2. Assemble System: Follow manufacturer instructions to install Black Berkey elements and optional PF-2 filters.
  3. Fill Upper Chamber: Pour untreated water into the upper chamber.
  4. Allow Filtration: Gravity pulls water through filters into the lower chamber.
  5. Dispense Water: Use the spigot to fill cups or bottles.
  6. Secure for Travel: Empty water before travel; secure system to prevent tipping.

7. Maintenance and Cleaning Best Practices

  • Clean filter elements every 6 months or when flow rate slows.
  • Use the included cleaning brush and mild soap.
  • Replace Black Berkey elements every 3,000 gallons or 2-3 years.
  • Sanitize chambers periodically with diluted bleach solution.
  • Store dry if not in use for extended periods.
