Short Queen vs. Standard Queen: Know Your RV Bed Size

Before you buy, measure your bed frame. Most RVs don't use standard queen sizes (60"x80"). Instead, they use:

  • Short Queen: 60" x 75" (most common)
  • Olympic Queen: 66" x 80" (in larger Class A or fifth wheels)
  • Custom sizes: Some beds have cut corners, radius edges, or odd widths.
